Before you start

Get Ready to Trade

You need a crypto wallet and USDT or USDC on Polygon to create an escrow. Follow these two steps—and watch the short videos—so you’re ready to use Fintdex.

1. Install MetaMask

MetaMask is the wallet you’ll connect to Fintdex. It holds your funds and lets you sign transactions securely. On desktop use the browser extension; on mobile, download the app.

  1. Desktop: Download the MetaMask extension for Chrome, Firefox, Brave, or Edge from metamask.io/download. Mobile: Install the MetaMask app from the App Store (iOS) or Play Store (Android).
  2. Open MetaMask and choose “Create a wallet.” Follow the setup steps.
  3. Store your 12-word recovery phrase very safely. Write it down on paper and keep it in a secure place. Never store it in email, cloud, or screenshots. Anyone with these 12 words can access your wallet and funds—never share them with anyone, including “support” or strangers.
  4. When you use Fintdex, connect your wallet and switch to Polygon Mainnet when prompted (Fintdex runs on Polygon).

Watch the video above, then download from metamask.io/download, App Store, or Play Store.

2. Get USDT or USDC on Polygon

Escrow on Fintdex uses stablecoins (USDT or USDC) on the Polygon network. You need to buy or receive them before creating an escrow.

  1. Make sure MetaMask is set to Polygon Mainnet (not Ethereum). Fintdex will prompt you to switch if needed.
  2. Buy USDT or USDC: when you create an escrow on Fintdex, if your balance is low, the app shows a “Buy stablecoins” option that opens providers like MoonPay, Ramp, or Onramp.money (for India).
  3. Complete the purchase with your card or bank; the tokens are sent to your MetaMask wallet on Polygon.
  4. Return to Fintdex Create Escrow and continue—your balance will update automatically.

Watch: How to get USDT or USDC in MetaMask. On Fintdex, use the in-app “Buy stablecoins” button to buy on Polygon.
